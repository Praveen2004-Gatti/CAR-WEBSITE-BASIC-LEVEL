Branded Car Inventory Overview

In the automotive industry, inventory refers specifically to branded cars that are available for sale. These include showroom-ready vehicles from well-known manufacturers such as BMW, Mercedes, Audi, and Toyota. This inventory consists of finished vehicles that are either in stock, on display, or reserved for customers.

Relevant Inventory Categories

  • Finished Goods – New, branded cars ready for customer delivery
  • Spare Stock – Additional units of high-demand models kept in reserve
  • Demo Units – Vehicles used for test drives and demonstrations
